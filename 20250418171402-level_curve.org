#+title: Level Curve

* Definition
The *level curves* of a [[id:87d42439-b03b-48be-84ab-2215b4733dd7][function]] \(f\) of two variables are the curves with equations \(f(x,y) = k\), where \(k\) is constant (in the range of \(f\)).
