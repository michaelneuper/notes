#+title: Case Forms

* Nominative
Case that indicates the subject of a [[id:d1cfe60a-53d4-470a-aea5-4b4bc9890d8f][verb]].

/He/ likes her.

* Accusative
Case that indicates a direct object.

She hates /him/.

* Genitive
Case that indicates possession.

Ayanda broke /his/ heart.
