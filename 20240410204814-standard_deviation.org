#+title: Standard Deviation
#+filetags: random_variables

* Definition
#+begin_quote
The standard deviation is equal to the square root of [[id:3c1c1ebc-8580-4a58-b6f9-8d5fd1b1b33e][variance]], denoted

\begin{equation*}
\text{SD}(X) = \sqrt{\text{Var}(X)}
\end{equation*}
#+end_quote
