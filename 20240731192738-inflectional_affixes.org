#+title: Inflectional Affixes

* Definition
#+begin_quote
Inflectional affixes are said to have a specific [[id:6f9cb5ec-c73f-434f-9e55-c7a0ac95120f][grammatical]] function, such as changing the:
- tense
- number
- person
- possession/case
- aspect
- degree of comparison
#+end_quote

* Examples
- dog-/s/ - number (plural)
- person-/'s/ - possession
- teach-/ing/ - aspect
