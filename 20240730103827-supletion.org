#+title: Suppletion

* Definition
#+begin_quote
An inflection technique where the morpheme changes instead of adding an [[id:afd36133-0af4-41f7-a158-cc74636e0e6c][affix]].
#+end_quote

* Total Supppletion
- bad - worse
- good - better
- go - went
- is - are

* Partial Suppletion
- was - were
- teach - taught
