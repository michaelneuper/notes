#+title: Glossing

* Convention
Lines:
1. Source expression in the original language (in italics)
2. Gloss -> literal, word-for-word translation of the source expression
3. Idiomatic English translation of the expression (in double quotation marks)

* Example
| /Ek/ | /is/ | /lief/ | /vir/ | /jou./ |
|------+------+--------+-------+--------|
| I    | is   | love   | for   | you    |
"I love you"
