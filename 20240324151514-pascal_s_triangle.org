#+title: Pascal's Triangle

Using [[id:191ab052-9880-4ccb-be2d-85e052fe32bc][combinations]], we can compute the \(n\)'th row (where \(n \ge 0\)) of Pascal's Triangle.
\begin{equation*}
{n \choose 0} {n \choose 1} \cdots {n \choose n-1}{n \choose n}
\end{equation*}
