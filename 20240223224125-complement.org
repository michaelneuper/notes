#+title: Complement (set theory)

* Definition
- For sets \(A\) and \(U\), the complement of \(A\) is defined as all the elements [[id:86afe72f-5d27-4bd7-8ccc-1d599ee00835][NOT]] in \(A\)
  \begin{equation*}
        A^c = \{x\in U | x\not\in A\}
  \end{equation*}
- Also denoted \(\bar A\)

* Example
- If \(A = \{1,2,3\}\) and \(U = \{3,4\}\) then
- \(A^{c} = \{4\}\)
