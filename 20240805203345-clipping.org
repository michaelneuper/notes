#+title: Clipping

* Definition
#+begin_quote
New words formed by cutting off (or *clipping*) a part of the word (non-[[id:afd36133-0af4-41f7-a158-cc74636e0e6c][affixes]]).
#+end_quote

* Examples
- (tele)phone
- exam(ination)
- (re)fridge(rator)
