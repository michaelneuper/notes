#+title: Proof by Counter-example

* Definition
To disprove \(\forall x\in A, \alpha(x)\) (or equivalently, to prove \(\neg\forall x\in A, \alpha(x)\)), we demonstrate the existance of some \(a\in A\) for which \(\alpha(x)\) does not hold.
