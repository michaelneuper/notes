#+title: Prime Numbers
#+filetags: fundamentals

* Definition
#+begin_quote
A number \(p > 1\) in \(\mathbb{Z}^+\) is called *prime* if the only positive integers that [[id:8eace0e2-6a16-422e-a332-713fc01d4ea8][divide]] \(p\) are \(1\) and \(p\).
#+end_quote
