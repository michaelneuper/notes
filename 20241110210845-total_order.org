#+title: Total Order

* Definition
A [[id:e64ef180-2fff-4790-bf03-1920ea80b239][poset]] \(P\) is called a *total order* if for any two elements \(x, y \in P\) we have that either \(x \le_{P} y\) or \(y \le_{P} x\).

That is, any two elements in \(P\) are comparable.
