#+title: Derivational Morphemes

* Definition
#+begin_quote
Can change the meaning of a word.
Can (but doesn't always) change the class of a word.
#+end_quote

* Examples
- motive - noun
- motiv-/ate/ - verb
- motivatino-/al/ - adjective
