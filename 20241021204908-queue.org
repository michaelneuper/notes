#+title: Queue
#+filetags: data_structures

* Definition
#+begin_quote
A queue is an [[id:64ffa106-ed4a-4abf-983e-21f2fbe1852e][ADT]] which uses the FIFO (First In First Out) principle.
#+end_quote

* Operations
*** Enqueue
Add an item to the queue.
*** Dequeue
Remove an item to the queue.
