#+title: Simple Sentence

* Definition
A sentence that contains only one [[id:6855ed0e-8cd9-4f5e-ad8f-0b8dd3ec81e5][clause]] and one [[id:22dabe8b-5aec-443f-88eb-c23a4e8c728c][verb phrase]].

* Example
He packs the books onto the bookshelf.
