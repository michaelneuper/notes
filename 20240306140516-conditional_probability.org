#+title: Conditional Probability
#+filetags: conditional_probability

* Definition
The *conditional probability* of an event \(E\), given an event \(F\) with \(P(F) > 0\), is

\begin{equation*}
P(E|F) = \frac{P(E\cap F)}{P(F)}
\end{equation*}
