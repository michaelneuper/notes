#+title: Weight Matrix

* Definition
\begin{equation*}
W(G) : w_{ij} =
\begin{cases}
0 & \text{if } i = j \\
w(v_i, v_j) & \text{if } i \neq j \text{ and } v_i v_j \in E(G) \\
\infty & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
\end{equation*}
