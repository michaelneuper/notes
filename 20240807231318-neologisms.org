#+title: Neologisms

* Definition
#+begin_quote
New words that are in common use but have not been "accepted" as mainstream or entered into a dictionary.

May be new words for existing concepts or phenomena or words for new ones.
#+end_quote

* Examples
- saddict (n.)
- figital (adj.)
