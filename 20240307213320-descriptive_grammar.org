#+title: Descriptive Grammar

- Scientific description of speaker's [[id:f9efe42e-017f-4ede-9406-ab0526729e43][grammatical competence]]
- Concrete model of mental capacity
- Consists of finite number of rules and principles and a [[id:76abe154-3c50-40ed-896b-69bb6ccc7592][lexicon]]
- Describes the characteristics of a given language, those which distinguish that language from others
- Describes the rules and principles that are spontaneously acquired
- Constructed by grammarians in the study field of (Descriptive) Grammar
