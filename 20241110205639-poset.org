#+title: Poset

* Definition
An *order-reflecting map* of [[id:e64ef180-2fff-4790-bf03-1920ea80b239][posets]] \(P\) and \(Q\) is a function \(f: P \to Q\) which satisfies
\begin{equation*}
\forall x, y \in P,\, f(x) \le_{Q} f(y) \Longrightarrow x\le_{P} y
\end{equation*}
